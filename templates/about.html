About Us

The news of all news, all made by a certain furry friend.

Squirrel News is an independent digital magazine dedicated to bringing you stories that are worth reading. We believe that good journalism should be curious, honest, and a little bit fearless — much like the small, tenacious creatures that inspire our name.

Our Story

Squirrel News was founded on a simple frustration: too much noise, not enough substance. In a world where headlines are written to provoke rather than inform, we wanted to build something different. A place where stories are researched thoroughly, written with care, and presented without agenda. We started small — just a handful of writers with a shared passion for storytelling — and we have been growing ever since.

Our Mission

We are here to dig up the stories that matter. Whether it is a global event shaping the course of history or a quiet local moment that deserves to be celebrated, Squirrel News treats every story with the same level of dedication and respect. We do not chase clicks. We chase truth.

Our Team

Behind Squirrel News is a small but mighty team of writers, editors, and creators who genuinely love what they do. We come from different backgrounds and different corners of the world, but we are united by a belief that words have power and that stories, told well, can change the way people see things.

Join the Journey

Squirrel News is still young, and we are excited about everything that lies ahead. New stories, new voices, and new ideas are always on the horizon. We are glad you found us, and we hope you stick around — there is always another acorn to uncover.
